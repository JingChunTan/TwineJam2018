This is a highly linear structure: players are funneled towards the same place, which allows you to write towards one strong ending. Every choice that doesn't bring the player closer to that ideal ending either sets them back (perhaps forcing them to re-encounter enemies), or ends the story entirely (usually by sending the player to some horrible doom).
